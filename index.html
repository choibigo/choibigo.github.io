About Me

Hello!
I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Hanyang University, advised by Prof. Yoon-seon Oh (2023-Present).

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of Robotics and Computer Vision.

  • Scene Understanding: Semantic interpretation of surroundings.
  • Bimanual Manipulation: Control strategies for dual-arm systems.
  • Task Planning: Decomposing high-level human instructions.

About Me

Hello! I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Hanyang University, advised by Prof. Yoon-seon Oh (2023–Present).

Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a Computer Vision Researcher at Mirero System (2020–2023).
I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Jeonbuk National University (2015–2020).

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of Robotics and Computer Vision. I am particularly focused on human-friendly robot intelligence, with the ultimate goal of bridging the gap for robots to be seamlessly integrated into our domestic environments.


  • Scene Understanding: Enabling robots to semantically interpret their surroundings and understand the context of the environment they are placed in.
  • Bimanual Manipulation: Researching precise control strategies for dual-arm systems to physically execute complex tasks required by human instructions.
  • Task Planning: Developing algorithms that decompose high-level human instructions into executable sequences of robotic actions.
